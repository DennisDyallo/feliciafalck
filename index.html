Where Nature
Meets Song

"Music that weaves together Nordic melancholy and the warmth of soulful, heartfelt melodies."

Felicia Falck is a Swedish singer-songwriter known for her crystalline voice and heartfelt storytelling. Rooted in the granite landscapes of North Bohuslän, she crafts music that transforms the delicate and the raw into something that touches the soul.


Her sound is born from a deep connection to nature and her travels around the world, weaving together the tradition of Swedish "Visor" with warm, soulful melodies. Whether performing intimate acoustic sets or facilitating vocal workshops, Felicia creates a space of healing and authentic expression.

Live Experiences

Performances and workshops for festivals, studios, and cultural centers.

The Acoustic Journey

Intimate, stripped-back, and deeply moving. Felicia performs with her guitar, sharing stories behind the lyrics in a raw storytelling format.

Ideal for: Living room concerts, garden concerts, yoga studios, intimate festivals.

Full Band Experience

A rich, organic soundscape featuring talented folk musicians creating a full, layered live experience. Perfect for larger stages.

Ideal for: Folk festivals, outdoor and indoor stages, summer concerts.

"Find Your Sound" Workshop

Beyond performance, Felicia guides groups to overcome the fear of expression, finding their unique sound through improvisation and connection exercises.

Ideal for: Retreats, festivals, choirs, community centers.

1-on-1 Voice Coaching

Personal sessions either online or in person where Felicia helps you unlock your authentic voice. A safe, nurturing space to explore expression, build confidence, and connect with your inner creativity.

Ideal for: Anyone seeking personal vocal and creative development.
