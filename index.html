Songs from the Granite & the Heart

"Music that bridges the gap between Nordic melancholy and the warmth of Latin American rhythms."

Felicia Falck is a Swedish singer-songwriter known for her crystalline voice and heartfelt storytelling. Rooted in the granite landscapes of North Bohuslän, she crafts music that transforms the delicate and the raw into something that touches the soul.

Her sound is born from a deep connection to nature and her travels in Colombia, weaving together the tradition of Swedish "Visor" with the spiritual depth of medicine music. Whether performing intimate acoustic sets or facilitating vocal workshops, Felicia creates a space of healing and authentic expression.

Live Experiences

Performances and workshops for festivals, studios, and cultural centers.

The Acoustic Journey

Intimate, stripped-back, and deeply moving. Felicia performs with her guitar, sharing stories behind the lyrics in a raw storytelling format.

Ideal for: Living room concerts, yoga studios, intimate festivals.

Full Band Experience

A rich, organic soundscape featuring top-tier folk musicians on double bass, percussion, and accordion. Perfect for larger stages.

Ideal for: Folk festivals, outdoor stages, summer concerts.

"Free Your Voice" Workshop

Beyond performance, Felicia guides groups to overcome the fear of expression, finding their unique sound through improvisation and connection exercises.

Ideal for: Retreats, choirs, community centers.
